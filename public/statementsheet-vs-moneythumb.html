The short version

MoneyThumb has been converting statements since 2015 and its strength is format breadth: QBO, QFX, OFX, QIF and CSV, aimed squarely at QuickBooks and Quicken users. It comes as online plans (from $24.95/month for 5 statement conversions) or desktop applications with lifetime licenses ($599.95–$699.95).

Credit where due: MoneyThumb's desktop converters process files locally on your machine, like we do — their privacy story for desktop is legitimate. The differences are cost model, convenience, and verification: StatementSheet is $19 in the browser with no install and a penny-exact verification gate; MoneyThumb desktop is a $600 lifetime tool with far more output formats.

Choose MoneyThumb if…

  • You need QBO/QFX files for QuickBooks Desktop or Quicken — that's their specialty and we don't produce those formats.
  • You convert statements constantly and a lifetime desktop license amortizes well ($600 ≈ 32 of our batches).
  • You need OCR for scanned documents (their PDF+ add-on).

Choose StatementSheet if…

  • You need Excel or CSV — which QuickBooks Online, Xero, and every spreadsheet workflow accept — and you'd rather pay $19 than $600 or a monthly plan.
  • You want local processing without installing anything or managing per-user licenses: open the page, convert, done. Even on a locked-down work machine.
  • You want to verify the privacy claim yourself instead of trusting an installer: our code is public and the site works in airplane mode.

The honest FAQ

Isn't MoneyThumb desktop just as private?

For local processing, essentially yes — desktop software keeps files on your machine. The differences are auditability (our code is public and unminified; you can read what runs before it runs), zero install, and price. If you already own a MoneyThumb license and it works for you, keep using it.

Why doesn't StatementSheet export QBO?

Scope. Excel/CSV covers QuickBooks Online, Xero, and spreadsheet workflows, and keeping the tool small is what makes it auditable. If QBO demand is real, it's on the roadmap — email us and vote.
