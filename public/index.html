How it works

1. Drop your PDFs

Digital statements from any bank or card portal. The files open locally in your browser — exactly like opening them in a PDF viewer.

2. We verify, not guess

StatementSheet re-computes the running balance across every transaction and checks it against your statement's own opening and closing balances. If it doesn't tie to the penny, we tell you — and you don't pay for that statement.

3. Download your spreadsheet

One clean Excel workbook (transactions + per-statement summary) or CSV, ready for QuickBooks, Xero, or Excel. Dates normalized, debits negative, credit cards handled correctly.

Don't trust us — verify us

Most converters upload your statements to their servers. StatementSheet was built so that it can't see your data, and you can prove it in 60 seconds:

  1. The airplane test. Load this page, then disconnect your Wi-Fi. Conversion, verification, preview, and even spreadsheet generation still work — because nothing is sent anywhere.
  2. The browser's own referee. Open DevTools → Network tab, then convert a statement. You'll see zero requests during processing. Our Content-Security-Policy header instructs your browser to block this page from transmitting data anywhere except Stripe checkout.
  3. Read the code. The full source is public and unminified. What runs is what you read.

FAQ

Which banks are supported?

Any bank or credit card that issues a digital PDF with a transaction table — checking, savings, and card statements, including separate deposits/withdrawals sections and multi-line descriptions. Because every result is balance-verified, you'll always know immediately if your bank's layout isn't parsed correctly (and you won't pay for it).

Are scanned statements supported?

Not yet. StatementSheet works with digital PDFs downloaded from your bank's website or app. Scanned/photographed statements are on the roadmap.

What exactly gets sent over the network?

During conversion: nothing. If you buy a download, your browser goes to Stripe's checkout page (like any online purchase), and afterwards this site asks Stripe "was this session paid?" — a request that contains a Stripe session ID and nothing else. Your files and transactions never leave your machine.

Is my payment information safe?

Payments are handled entirely by Stripe; we never see your card number. We have no user accounts and no database.

What do I get in the download?

An .xlsx workbook with a Transactions sheet (date, description, amount, balance, source file, verification status) and a Summary sheet (per statement: opening, closing, net, balance check result) — plus a CSV with the same transaction data.
