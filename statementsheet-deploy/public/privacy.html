Privacy Policy

The short version: we never receive your files.

StatementSheet processes your PDF statements entirely within your web browser, on your device. Your files, and the transactions inside them, are never uploaded to, transmitted to, or stored on our servers or any third party's servers. A Content-Security-Policy header directs your browser to block this site from sending data anywhere other than our payment-verification endpoint (which receives only a Stripe session ID).

What we do receive: if you purchase a download, Stripe (our payment processor) collects your payment details under Stripe's privacy policy. We receive from Stripe only confirmation that a checkout session was paid. Standard web server logs (IP address, page requested, timestamp) are kept by our hosting provider for security purposes.

What we don't do: no analytics trackers on the converter, no advertising pixels, no user accounts, no database of customer data, no cookies other than what Stripe requires for checkout.

Your spreadsheet is generated in your browser and saved directly to your device. We never see it.

Questions: support@statementsheet.com
